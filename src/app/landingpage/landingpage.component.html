Welcome to MQL!

Our web app uses aspects of Gamification and targeted psychological feedback to strengthen your mental health and motivation.

Through gamification, everyday tasks are transformed into exciting quests that increase your motivation with playful elements and rewards.

Psychological feedback promotes mindfulness and self-efficacy, supports positive behavioral changes, and strengthens your mental resilience.



Create your character and receive tailored quests that are matched to your assigned class. MQL offers you an intuitive user interface with RPG elements and tasks to increase your motivation and mindfulness. Open chests to reward yourself after completing your productive quests!
